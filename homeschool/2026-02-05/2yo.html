Village Homeschool

Age 2 (Toddler) - February 5, 2026

Daily Inspiration

Word of the Day: Luminary - a person who inspires others.

This Day in History: In 1919, famous movie stars Charlie Chaplin and friends started "United Artists" to make their own movies.

Faith-Based Learning

Scripture of the Day: James 1:5 - "If any of you lack wisdom, let him ask of God..."

Faith Thought & Activity: Heavenly Father loves to hear us talk to Him! Show a picture of a child praying. Activity: Help your toddler fold their hands and practice saying a very short "Thank you" prayer for one thing they like (like a toy or a snack).

Academic Content

Math

Color Sorting: Gather 5-10 small objects of two different colors (e.g., red and blue blocks). Help your toddler separate them into two piles. Count "one, two" for each.

History

My Family: Look at a family photo together. Point to people and name them. "That's Grandma! That's Grandpa!" Talk about where they live.

Science

Sink or Float: Fill a small bowl with water. Take a plastic toy and a metal spoon. Ask, "Will it sink or float?" Drop them in and watch!

Reading

Picture Time: Read a short board book about animals. Every time you see an animal, make the sound it makes (Woof, Meow, Moo) and encourage them to copy you.

Writing

Salt Tray Tracing: Put a thin layer of salt on a cookie sheet. Use a finger to draw circles and lines. It's like writing in the sand!
