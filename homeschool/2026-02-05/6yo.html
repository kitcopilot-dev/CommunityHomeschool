Village Homeschool

Age 6 (Kindergarten/1st) - February 5, 2026

Daily Inspiration

Word of the Day: Luminary - a person who shines brightly and inspires others.

This Day in History: In 1919, United Artists was formed by movie legends including Charlie Chaplin. They wanted to have the freedom to tell their own stories!

Faith-Based Learning

Scripture of the Day: James 1:5 - "If any of you lack wisdom, let him ask of God, that giveth to all men liberally, and upbraideth not; and it shall be given him."

Faith Thought & Activity: Just like Joseph Smith, we can ask Heavenly Father for help when we don't know what to do. Activity: Think of one thing you want to learn or a question you have. Kneel down and say a prayer asking for "wisdom" or help to learn it today.

Academic Content

Math

Addition Within 20: Solve these problems using buttons or blocks if you need help:

  • 8 + 4 = ?
  • 10 + 5 = ?
  • 7 + 7 = ?

History

Symbols of Freedom: Talk about the American Flag or the Statue of Liberty. Why are these symbols important to our country? Draw a picture of your favorite one.

Science

Living vs. Non-Living: Take a 5-minute walk. Find 3 things that are alive (like a tree or a bug) and 3 things that are not alive (like a rock or a fence). Why are they different?

Reading

Reading Practice: Read a short story book of your choice. Find 5 words in the book that you can spell on your own. Write them down.

Writing

My Favorite Animal: Write 3 complete sentences about an animal you love. Remember to start with a capital letter and end with a period!
