üßí Age 9 (4th Grade)

Faith Thought: Being an example isn't just about what you don't do; it's about the kind things you DO. Think of one person who needs a "light" today.

Math: 3-Digit Subtraction with Regrouping. Complete 10 practice problems involving "borrowing" across zeros.

Writing: Narrative Writing: Write a story about a time you felt brave enough to do the right thing, even if it was hard.

üëß Age 6 (1st Grade)

Faith Thought: You are like a little candle! When you are kind, your light gets brighter and makes people happy.

Math: Addition to 10. Draw 5 suns, then 3 more. Count how many suns there are altogether.

History: Meet Augustus. He was a leader from a very long time ago in a place called Rome. He was called the "Father" of his country.
