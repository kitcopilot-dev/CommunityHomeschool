Village Homeschool Plan

Thursday, February 5, 2026 | Age 2

Faith & Scripture

Scripture of the Day: "Adam fell that men might be; and men are, that they might have joy." (2 Nephi 2:25)

Faith Thought: Heavenly Father wants us to be happy! Let's sing "If You're Happy and You Know It" and clap our hands.

Academic Assignments
  • Math: Group two of your favorite toys together. Can you count them? "One, Two!"
  • Science: "Soft vs. Hard" - Find a soft teddy bear and a hard building block. Touch them both.
  • Reading: Look at a picture book with animal photos. Point to the "moo" cow!
  • Writing: Practice holding a big crayon and making happy scribbles on a paper.
Daily Inspiration

Word of the Day: Joy - A very big happy feeling!

This Day in History: Long ago, a leader named Caesar Augustus was called the "Father of his Country."
