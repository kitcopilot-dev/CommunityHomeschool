Village Homeschool Plan

Thursday, February 5, 2026 | Age 6 (1st Grade)

Faith & Scripture

Scripture of the Day: "Adam fell that men might be; and men are, that they might have joy." (2 Nephi 2:25)

Faith Thought: Our Heavenly Father's plan is a "Plan of Happiness." Even when things are hard, Jesus helps us find joy. Activity: Draw a picture of one thing that made you smile today.

Academic Assignments
  • Math: Practice addition! What is 10 + 5? What is 8 + 4? Use cereal pieces to count if you need help.
  • History: The Statue of Liberty is a big green statue in New York. It stands for "Freedom." Why is freedom important?
  • Science: What is a "Mammal"? Mammals have hair or fur and give their babies milk. Is a dog a mammal? Is a bird a mammal?
  • Reading: Read one short story book out loud to a parent or a sibling.
  • Writing: Write the following sentence in your best handwriting: "I can choose to be happy today."
Daily Inspiration

Word of the Day: Cognizant - To be aware of something or to know it.

This Day in History: In 1919, four famous movie stars started their own company called "United Artists" so they could make the movies they wanted to make.
