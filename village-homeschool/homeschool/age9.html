Math

The "Welcome Stranger" gold nugget found today in 1869 weighed 71.08 kg. If gold costs $65 per gram, how much would that nugget be worth today? (Hint: 1 kg = 1000 grams).

History

The Fall of Carthage: In 146 BCE, the Third Punic War ended with the destruction of Carthage by the Roman Republic. Research why the Romans and Carthaginians were such bitter rivals. (Hint: It involves the Mediterranean Sea).

Science

Density: Gold is one of the densest elements. A small cube of gold is much heavier than a same-sized cube of iron. Find three objects in your house and guess which one is the most dense.

Reading

Read a chapter of your current book. Identify the main conflict. How is the protagonist showing perseverance?

Writing

Journal Entry: Imagine you were the person who found the "Welcome Stranger" gold nugget in Australia in 1869. Write a diary entry describing the moment you saw it in the dirt.
