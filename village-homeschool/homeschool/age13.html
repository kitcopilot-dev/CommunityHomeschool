Thursday, February 5, 2026 | Age: 13 (8th Grade)
Word of the Day: PERSEVERANCE (Continued effort to do or achieve something despite difficulties, failure, or opposition.)
"I will go and do the things which the Lord hath commanded, for I know that the Lord giveth no commandments unto the children of men, save he shall prepare a way for them..." — 1 Nephi 3:7
Faith Thought: This scripture is often used to show God's power, but notice Nephi's proactive stance: "I will go and do." God provides the "way," but we provide the "feet." How can you apply this balance of faith and action to your personal goals this week?
Activity: Study the context of 1 Nephi chapters 3 and 4. List the obstacles Nephi faced and how his perseverance led to the "way" being opened.

Writing

Essay Task: "The Psychology of Resilience." Using 1 Nephi 3:7 as a spiritual foundation and a historical figure (like Buster Keaton, whose film premiered today, or a WWII general) as a case study, write a 300-word essay on why perseverance is more critical than talent.
