Village Homeschool Plan

Thursday, February 5, 2026 | Age 9 (4th Grade)

Faith & Scripture

Scripture of the Day: "Adam fell that men might be; and men are, that they might have joy." (2 Nephi 2:25)

Faith Thought: "Joy" is different from just "fun." True joy comes from the Spirit. How can you help someone else feel joy today? Activity: Write a short note to a family member telling them why they bring you joy.

Academic Assignments
  • Math: Multi-digit multiplication. Solve: 124 x 3 and 45 x 6. Show your work!
  • History: The California Gold Rush (1849). Thousands of people moved west hoping to get rich. If you lived then, would you have gone? Why or why not?
  • Science: The Solar System. List all 8 planets in order from the sun. Which one is the "Red Planet"?
  • Reading: Read 20 minutes of your current chapter book. Write a 2-sentence summary of what happened.
  • Writing: Write a poem (at least 4 lines) about what "Joy" feels like to you.
Daily Inspiration

Word of the Day: Cognizant - Having knowledge or being aware of something. Example: "She was cognizant of the rules before the game started."

This Day in History: In 1917, Mexico adopted its current constitution, which is the set of rules their government follows.
