Village Homeschool Plan

Thursday, February 5, 2026 | Age 13 (8th Grade)

Faith & Scripture

Scripture of the Day: "Adam fell that men might be; and men are, that they might have joy." (2 Nephi 2:25)

Faith Thought: Lehi is teaching his son Jacob about the necessity of opposition. Without "bitter," we wouldn't know "sweet." Activity: Read 2 Nephi 2:27. How does our "agency" (the power to choose) relate to our ability to have joy?

Academic Assignments
  • Math: Pre-Algebra / Algebra. Solve for x: 4(x - 2) + 10 = 22.
  • History: The Great Depression. Research the "Dust Bowl." How did environmental factors combine with economic ones to make life difficult in the 1930s?
  • Science: Biology/Botany. Explain the process of Photosynthesis. What are the three things a plant needs to create glucose?
  • Reading: Read 30 minutes of your current novel. Identify one example of "foreshadowing" in the text.
  • Writing: Write a 200-word response: In 1919, United Artists was formed by actors who wanted more control over their art. Why is "creative independence" important in the entertainment industry?
Daily Inspiration

Word of the Day: Cognizant - Having knowledge or being aware of something; mindful. Example: "The pilot was cognizant of the changing weather patterns."

This Day in History: In 2 BC, the Roman Senate granted Caesar Augustus the title "Pater Patriae," meaning "Father of the Country."
